Governance and DAO

Participate in the DAO and get rewarded

BSC Pixels is designed to be a community driven initiative and to fufill that, the project will become a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) durign the final epoch where block holders will receive governance tokens (in the form of NFTs) an can participate in the governance of the overall project.

Token holders may vote on different measures of the project from increasing wall size, allocating DAO fund to buying back blocks at prices voted upon and burning them, voting on a new investment vehicle for the DAO fund or suggesting major ad campaigns (eg. buying a billboard at timessquare).

Block holders aspiring for more clout in the DAO may strive for the position of DIRECTOR which requires holding at minimum 50 blocks. Directors may propose new motions and will receive DIRECTOR level NFTs signifying their major invovlement in the DAO.
